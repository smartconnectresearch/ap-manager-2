8 Manager Programme FAQ

How is this different from being an affiliate?

As a manager, you earn on two levels: your personal referrals (10% + 5%) AND a 2.5% override on all team revenue. Plus, you get enhanced support, tools, and direct access to SCR leadership. Affiliates typically earn £20-50k; managers earn £50-100k+.

What if I've never managed a team before?

Many successful managers had no prior team management experience. What matters is your network, business acumen, and willingness to guide others. We provide all the training, templates, and ongoing support you need to succeed.

How quickly can I start earning?

Most managers close their first personal referral within 30 days and have their first team member productive within 60 days. Commissions are paid within 30 days of client payment. Many managers see their first £5,000+ commission within 90 days.

What investment is required?

Zero financial investment. No joining fees, monthly charges, or hidden costs. Your only investment is 10+ hours per month of your time. We provide all training, tools, and support at no cost. You earn from Day 1.

Can I do this alongside my current role?

Yes! Most managers start part-time while maintaining their primary income. The 10-hour monthly commitment is designed to fit around a busy schedule. Many transition to full-time once their SCR income exceeds their salary.

What makes SCR different from other programmes?

Three things: 1) Our 90% client retention means recurring commissions, 2) Average project values of £150k mean bigger commissions, 3) We handle everything after introduction - you never have to sell, deliver, or manage clients. This is passive income at its best.
